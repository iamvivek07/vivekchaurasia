RESTAURANT
UPPER DECK

An à la carte restaurant offering a seasonal menu inspired by a passion for fresh, local ingredients with an outdoor deck where our guests can enjoy lunch or dinner. We will re-introduce the old fashioned attitude of enjoying a pre-dinner aperitif by the bar at The Captain’s Cabin prior to heading at the dinner table as well as after dinner cocktails. A vast range of Wines of the World and other deluxe beverages.

PUB
LOWER DECK

The Pub is housed on the lower level of the vessel offering all day hospitality, streaming sports, cultural recreation programmes and other scheduled events. Here we serve a range of Ales, Lagers, Stouts, Ciders, Special Brews both on drought and bottled, cocktail carte offering specific spirit-varied cocktail listings, aperitifs, deluxe spirits, and non-alcoholic beverages. One may enjoy with a cup of tea or a piece of cake.
